# Statement of Work

## Overview
Create a card to display the total or net profit for the current filtered time period

## Objective
The goal is to provide users with a clear visualization of the total or net profit dynamically based on their selected filters within the given time period.

## Deliverables
- A card in the user interface displaying total/net P&L values.

## Constraints
- Must dynamically reflect the filtered time period.

## Assumptions
- Relevant data is accessible through existing APIs or backend endpoints.
